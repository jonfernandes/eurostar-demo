INC0006234 EPP voucher expiry email sent even though voucher has been redeemed

PNR : SSAPLV
passenger name : WEIGHT
EMAIL-PHILWEIGHT@GOFAST.CO.UK

Steps Taken : Customer has a £20 epp voucher valid until 06/08/2013.
Customer redeems voucher on 14/05/2013 against a booking and voucher is set as redeemed in datacash evoucher tool.

Results Received : When the voucher should have expired on 6th August, the customer received an email to state that his e-voucher has now expired and cannot be used, even though it has already been redeemed against a booking in May.

Results Expected : Customer should not receive this expiry email if the voucher has already been redeemed against a booking.
