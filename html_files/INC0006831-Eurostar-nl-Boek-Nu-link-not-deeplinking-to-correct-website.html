INC0006831 Eurostar.nl 'Boek Nu' link not deeplinking to correct website

Website: Eurostar.nl
Browser: unknown 
PNR: N/A
 
On the Eurostar.nl website if a customer clicks 'Boek Nu' they are taken to http://www.eurostar.nl/booking-redirect.html which gives 2 options - Brussels to London & Any Belgium Station to London.
 
Both link directly to www.eurostar.com/be-nl/ instead of deep linking to the website with the right station prefilled correctly.
