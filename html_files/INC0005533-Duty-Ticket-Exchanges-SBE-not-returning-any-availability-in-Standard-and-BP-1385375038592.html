INC0005533 [Duty Ticket Exchanges] SBE not returning any availability in Standard and BP

CURRENT RESULT When attempting to exchange a duty ticket SBE/WDI do only return fares & availability in Standard Premier.

EXPECTED RESULT When attempting to exchange a duty ticket SBE/WDI should return fares & availability in Standard, Standard Premier and Business Premier.
For Standard I would expect the fare BFSASB to be returned on the 08:35 train.
