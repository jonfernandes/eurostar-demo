INC0005086 Changing outbound weekly date impacts inbound date

Perform a search, London to Paris, 1x Adult, Outbound 10/7  Inbound 10/7
 
1) Select the Outbound train - 10/7 @ 05:40
2) Visit Inbound page (don't select any train)
3) Use back button / click back on Outbound leg
4) Click on Thurs 11th Jul in the weekly view
5) Click on Wed 10th Jul in the weekly view
6) Click Continue (without selecting a train as you already have the 05:40 in your basket)
7) On the Inbound page the date is now defaulted to Thurs 11th Jul
Customer never notices it's switched from the 10th to the 11th and books the wrong dates.
 
It seems the system automatically moves the inbound date to make sure it is equal or greater than the outbound date (which makes sense) but once you select a date beyond the inbound date it gets changed to be equal but never reverts back if you then change your outbound date back.
 
We have an example of this exact scenario happening on PNR: SDKIBT
