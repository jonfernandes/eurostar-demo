INC0005710 EFT/EPP Booking Statement on IE10 shows no 'Show/Hide details' options CLOSED

FIX DUE V 1.672.0 28.8.13 SUSPEND

Customers on Internet Explorer 10 on Windows 7 are unable to see the 'Show Details' tab on their Booking Statement, this results in them not being able to see their evoucher numbers.
