INC0005473 Unable to book with card expiring on or past the month of the travel date.CLOSED

Browser: Any
Market: UK
If you try to book a ticket online for June but your card expires in May you will receive an error about having an incorrect expiry date.
If you try to book for June and your card expires in June you will also receive an error about having an incorrect expiry date.

This should not be the case, passengers should be able to purchase tickets with an expiring card.Â
