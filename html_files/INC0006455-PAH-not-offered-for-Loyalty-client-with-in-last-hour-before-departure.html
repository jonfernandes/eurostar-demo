INC0006455 PAH not offered for Loyalty client with in last hour before departure

Scenario:
1 ) EFT redemption booking made on dotcom done at 14.09 :
         - Train departing within the hour (i.e. 14.52).
         - Train departing from London (to Paris)
2) the print at home link not available at the end of the booking and on the after sale section
 
Note this is also happening for the French agencies who try to retrieve their print a home on the website using Manage your booking
 
STEPS TO REPRODUCE:
 
Book a one way from London - Paris (for instance) and then try to issue the print at home less then 1 h before departure. 
 
Expected result: It should work.
 
I would recommend you make a booking with no 48h PAH restriction (such as a booking with an EFT member or booked with EFT points).
 
PLEASE REMEMBER .. IT DOESNT MATTER WHEN THE BOOKING IS MADE .. IT MATTERS THAT THE PAH NEEDS TO BE SEEN RIGHT UP TO DEPARTURE .
