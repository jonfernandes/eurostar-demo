Hi Team, 
 
We have investigated this issue with the latest example provided by EDHD (EPP member 30838110005978830) and we were able to reproduce this issue. 
 
During our investigation, we got to know that the <customerTransaction> response which we are getting from iloyal in that, we are getting <point type> PNDPNT </pointType> which indicates "pending point" . Because of this in the online statement the number of points is displayed in blue with the pending points hover text. 
 
This incident has been assigned to iLoyal development team for further investigation.
