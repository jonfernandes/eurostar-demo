EURO-1908 Mismatching Booking Error

Train + Hotel (Book&Save) booking made on www.eurostar.com The train has been confirmed for different dates than the hotel. For example, the train has been confirmed for 19th to 21st of February, the hotel has been confirmed for 26th to 27th of February. Reported to Orbitz. Examples Needed. WORKAROUND: Train booked incorrectly: Exchange train booking for correct dates. Write off difference if necessary. Hotel booked incorrectly: Contact Orbitz through ADT to request change of dates.
