INC0005758 SBE/Eurostar.com mixing Warwick and Warwick Parkway in a availability request.CLOSED

When Warwick or Warwick Parkway is selected in UK Interlining through EUROSTAR.COM, the SBE offers on some journeys the incorrect departure or origin station.
In the attached example, the request was from Warwick Parkway to Paris but the journey at 1442 was from Warwick.
If the journey is requested to/from Warwick, the UK origin/destination must always be Warwick and if the is requested to/from Warwick Parkway, the UK origin/destination must always be Warwick Parkway.

These two stations are completely separate so should not be mixed.Â
